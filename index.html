Welcome to The Emotional Cup

A safe space for students to share their problems and get professional advice anonymously.

How It Works

Students can book counseling sessions anonymously. Parents are kept informed while respecting privacy. Our system matches students with the right therapist based on their needs.

When a student chooses to seek counseling, they will fill out a simple questionnaire. This helps us understand their specific challenges and preferences, allowing us to pair them with a therapist who specializes in their area of concern.

Students can schedule their sessions at their convenience. We offer both virtual and in-person appointments, ensuring support is easily accessible.

We prioritize confidentiality and provide a secure environment for students to express their thoughts and feelings freely.

Instructions

To get started, navigate to the 'Book a Session' page where you can choose your preferred day, date, and time. If you have questions, feel free to reach out through our 'Contact Us' page.

Book a Session

To book your session, please follow these steps:

  1. Select your preferred day from the dropdown menu.
  2. Choose a date using the date picker.
  3. Pick a time for your session.
  4. Click 'Confirm Booking' to finalize your session.

Need Help?

If you have any questions or need assistance with the counseling process, please reach out to our support team. We are here to help you every step of the way.
